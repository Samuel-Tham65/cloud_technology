New york off-broadway week

Wildfires blazing across eastern Canada have sent plumes of smoke cascading hundreds of miles south into the US, transforming huge swaths of the Northeast into a Martian landscape with hazy, red skies.The US government has issued air quality warnings across the Midwest and Northeast, urging people to limit their time outside as the smoke passes through. In New York City, the air smelled strongly of burning timber.
